About SAZUG Past Questions Portal

Empowering students with the resources they need for academic excellence and success

Who We Are

The SAZUG Past Questions Portal is a comprehensive digital academic resource platform designed specifically for students of Sa’adu Zungur University, Bauchi. We provide organized, easy access to past examination questions for all faculties, departments, and academic levels.

Our mission is to simplify academic preparation by providing a structured platform where students can download relevant past questions, study effectively, and gain insight into exam formats and expectations. We believe that access to the right materials can make a significant difference in academic performance.

This project is a product of dedication, teamwork, and innovation by AGI – Aliyu Garba Innovations. We combined our skills in coding, research, design, and guidance to build a platform that benefits the entire student community.

Our Mission

To empower SAZUG students with easily accessible, well-organized past examination questions that enhance their study efficiency and academic performance.

Our Vision

To become the leading academic resource platform for Nigerian university students, supporting educational excellence through technology and innovation.
