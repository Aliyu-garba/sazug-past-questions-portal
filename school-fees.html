School Fees & Payment - SAZUG Freshers Guide

As a newly admitted student of Sa'adu Zungur University, Bauchi State (SAZUG), understanding the structure, procedure, and timing of school fees payment is crucial. Proper knowledge of this will prevent unnecessary delays, missed deadlines, and errors that could affect your academic status.

1. Categories of Fees at SAZUG

The total fees payable by students at SAZUG are broken into several categories:

  • Acceptance Fee: Paid immediately after admission confirmation via JAMB.
  • School Fees (Tuition): Paid at the beginning of each session.
  • Faculty/Departmental Dues: Paid at the faculty or departmental level.
  • Medical and Student Union Dues: Paid once per session during registration.
  • Other Charges: For ID card, ICT, matriculation gown, etc.

2. Current SAZUG School Fees Range (As of 2025)

School fees differ based on department and faculty. Here's an average range:

  • Faculty of Science & Technology: ₦45,000 – ₦60,000
  • Faculty of Arts & Education: ₦38,000 – ₦50,000
  • Faculty of Social Sciences: ₦40,000 – ₦55,000
  • Faculty of Law or Medical Sciences: ₦70,000 – ₦85,000 (subject to availability)
Note: These figures are subject to change. Always confirm from your faculty or portal before payment.

3. Step-by-Step Guide to School Fees Payment

  1. Log in to SAZUG Portal: Visit https://www.sazug.edu.ng and log in using your JAMB number.
  2. Generate Remita (RRR): Navigate to “Fees Payment” and generate a Remita number.
  3. Make Payment: You can pay using:
    • Online payment with ATM card (Visa/Mastercard)
    • Mobile banking app or bank transfer
    • Cash deposit at any commercial bank using your RRR
  4. Confirm Payment: After payment, return to the portal and click "Confirm Payment" to validate it.
  5. Print Receipt: Download and print your school fee receipt for clearance.

4. Late Payment & Penalties

Students are strongly advised to pay their fees before the official deadline. Late payments may attract penalties such as:

  • Extra charges added to your total fee
  • Inability to register courses
  • Disqualification from exams

5. How to Know If Your Payment Was Successful

  • Check your portal dashboard for "Payment Status: Successful"
  • Download your e-receipt or transaction history
  • If status is pending, visit ICT or contact portal support

6. Tips for Safe Payment

  • Always use your personal phone number and email.
  • Never pay through unauthorized agents or individuals.
  • Ensure internet connection is stable if paying online.
  • Keep your RRR and transaction reference safely.
Aliyu's advises: "Don’t delay fee payment. Once you’re admitted, prioritize payment to avoid stress."

7. Where to Get Help

For issues relating to school fees, visit the Bursary Unit or ICT office. You can also email the SAZUG support team or check announcements via your portal dashboard.

8. Final Word

Paying your fees on time is the key to unlocking access to all academic services including matriculation, exams, and course registration. SAZUG has made the process transparent and digital, allowing students to monitor everything via their portal. Stay proactive, ask questions, and always verify any instruction before acting.
