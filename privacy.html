Privacy & Data Protection

Your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Introduction

At SAZUG Past Questions Portal, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of your data. By using our platform, you trust us with your information, and we take that responsibility seriously.

What We Collect

We collect minimal information. Currently, SAZUG Past Questions Portal does not store personal data on external servers. Any information you enter during registration (such as name or email) is saved locally on your device only to personalize your experience. When we introduce online accounts and server-based features in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect that. We also use Google Analytics to collect anonymous information such as pages visited and browser type. This data helps us understand usage trends and improve the site experience:

Full Name

To personalize your experience and create your account

Email Address

To communicate important updates and secure your account

Password

To protect your account (encrypted and never stored in plain text)

We do not collect any additional personal information beyond these three essential data points. Our platform is designed with privacy in mind, minimizing data collection to only what is absolutely necessary.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account on our platform

  • To provide access to past questions and educational resources

  • To communicate important updates about our services

  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests

  • To ensure platform security and prevent unauthorized access

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction:

Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2+ protocols

Secure Storage

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage

We regularly review and update our security practices to ensure the ongoing protection of your information. Our team is trained in data protection best practices, and access to user data is strictly limited to authorized personnel only.

User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

Request a copy of your personal data

Right to Correction

Update inaccurate or incomplete information

Right to Deletion

Request deletion of your personal data

Right to Portability

Receive your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact Details section.
