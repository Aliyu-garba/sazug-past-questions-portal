Freshers Resources & Student Guide

Your comprehensive guide to navigating university life at SAZUG

Welcome to SAZUG!

Congratulations on your admission! This guide is designed to help you navigate your first year with ease. Below you'll find essential resources and information to get you started on the right track.

Registration & Matric Number

Follow the official steps to complete your registration. Collect your Matric Number from the department after confirmation.

School Fees & Payment

  • Visit the student portal to view fees schedule.
  • Make payments through the approved bank or portal.
  • Keep your payment receipts for clearance.

Accommodation

  • Apply for hostel early through the student affairs office.
  • Check notice boards for off-campus housing options.

Campus Navigation

Locate your faculty, department, ICT center, library, and admin block. Ask older students or use the map posted at major junctions.

Academic Resources

  • Register courses on time.
  • Attend your department's orientation program.
  • Check your email for official updates.

Rules, Dress Code & Discipline

Abide by campus rules. Dress modestly. Avoid exam malpractice and cultism.

Support & Communities

  • Join faculty/departmental WhatsApp or Telegram groups.
  • Visit student affairs office for guidance and support.

ICT & Biometric Capturing

Go to the ICT center for biometric data capturing. Take along your admission letter, school fees receipt, and passport photograph.

Extra Tips & Opportunities

  • Apply for available scholarships.
  • Attend skills workshops and school events.
  • Explore religious associations, student clubs, and volunteering groups.
